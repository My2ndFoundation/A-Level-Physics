# Acceleration

## Core Idea

Acceleration measures how quickly velocity changes.

## Symbol

`a`

## SI Unit

`m s⁻²`

## Scalar or Vector

Vector

## Definition

Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity with time.

## Related Equations

- `a = Δv / Δt`
- SUVAT equations under constant acceleration

## How It Is Measured

Acceleration can be determined from velocity-time data or motion sensor data.

## Graphical Meaning

On a velocity-time graph, acceleration is the gradient.
