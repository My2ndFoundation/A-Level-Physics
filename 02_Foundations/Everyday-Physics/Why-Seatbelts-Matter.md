# Why Seatbelts Matter

## Everyday Question

Why do seatbelts reduce injury in a car crash?

## Short Answer

A seatbelt increases the time over which your body slows down, reducing the force on you.

## Physical Explanation

In a sudden stop, your body tends to keep moving forward. A seatbelt applies a force that slows you down more safely than hitting the dashboard or windscreen.

## Common Wrong Intuition

A common wrong intuition is that the seatbelt stops the crash. It does not. It changes how your body slows down.
