# Force

## Core Idea

A force is a push or pull that can change the motion or shape of an object.

## Prior Knowledge Level

GCSE

## Meaning

Forces can make objects accelerate, decelerate, change direction, or deform.

## Why It Matters for A-Level Physics

A-Level mechanics depends on understanding resultant force, acceleration, free-body diagrams, and [[Newton-Second-Law]].

## Everyday Intuition

Pushing a trolley harder usually makes it accelerate more.

## Key Quantities

- Force
- Mass
- [[Acceleration]]

## Related Concepts

- Resultant force
- Weight
- Friction

## Related Laws or Results

- [[Newton-Second-Law]]

## Common Misconceptions

- Confusing force with motion
- Thinking an object needs a force to keep moving at constant velocity

## Bridge to A-Level

At A-Level, force becomes more vector-based and is analysed using free-body diagrams and equations.
