# Copyright Rules

This vault may ingest notes and references from official specifications, textbooks, past papers, practical guides, GCSE foundation material, frontier references, and other books.

The LLM must not reproduce copyrighted source material wholesale.

## Allowed

- Paraphrased summaries
- Formula-level excerpts
- Short definitions when necessary
- Page references
- Chapter references
- Exercise/question references
- Original worked examples
- Original explanations
- Links to source pages

## Not Allowed

- Full copied chapters
- Long copied textbook passages
- Full reproduced copyrighted worked examples
- Bulk copying of exercise sets
- Treating raw textbook files as publishable wiki pages

## Source Pages

Source pages should preserve provenance and extraction structure, not source text.

## Worked Examples

Worked examples should be original or paraphrased and should cite source question numbers when derived from a source.
