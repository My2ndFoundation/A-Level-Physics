## Knowledge Types

* Foundations: pre-GCSE, GCSE, and bridge-to-A-Level ideas
* Common-Sense Physics: intuitive everyday explanations
* Physical Quantities: measurable quantities, symbols, units, scalar/vector status
* Concepts: physical ideas and phenomena
* Laws and Results: equations, principles, conservation laws, named results
* Models: idealised systems and assumptions
* Methods: problem-solving and analysis techniques
* Representations: graphs, diagrams, notations, visual forms
* Experiments and Practicals: measurements, uncertainties, apparatus, graph analysis
* Applications: real and exam contexts
* Problems: worked examples, problem types, mistake patterns, extensions
* Frontier Maps: shallow maps of advanced and modern Physics
* Sources: source records and provenance
