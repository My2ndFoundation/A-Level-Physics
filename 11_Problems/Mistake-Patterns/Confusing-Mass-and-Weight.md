# Confusing Mass and Weight

## Mistake

A common mistake is treating mass and weight as the same physical quantity.

## Why It Happens

In everyday language, weight is often used when mass is meant.

## Example

Saying an object has a weight of `5 kg` is incorrect. Kilograms measure mass. Weight is a force measured in newtons.

## Correct Approach

Mass is measured in kilograms. Weight is a force and is calculated using `W = mg`.
